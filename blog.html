Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions Answered

Get quick answers to the most common questions about rolling shutters.

How long do rolling shutters last?
With proper maintenance, our shutters typically last 10-15 years. Premium materials and professional installation ensure longevity.
What's the difference between manual and motorized shutters?
Manual shutters are operated by hand and are cost-effective. Motorized shutters offer convenience with remote control and can include safety features.
How much does a rolling shutter cost?
Costs vary based on size, material, and type. Basic manual shutters start from ₹15,000, while premium motorized ones can range from ₹35,000-₹75,000.
Do you provide maintenance services?
Yes, we offer annual maintenance contracts, emergency repairs, and spare parts. Regular maintenance ensures smooth operation and extends lifespan.
How long does installation take?
Standard installations are completed within 2-3 days. Complex motorized installations may take 4-5 days depending on the requirements.
Can shutters be customized for specific requirements?
Absolutely! Every shutter is custom-built for your exact measurements. We can accommodate special sizes, colors, and features as needed.
