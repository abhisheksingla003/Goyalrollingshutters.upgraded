Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Founded in 2014 by Mr. Rajesh Goyal, Goyal Rolling Shutters started as a small family workshop in Mumbai's industrial area. What began as a passion for metalwork and precision engineering has grown into one of Mumbai's most trusted shutter manufacturing companies.

Over the past decade, we've installed over 500 shutters across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. Our commitment to quality, precision, and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to choice for shutter solutions in Mumbai and surrounding areas.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

Key moments that shaped our company and our commitment to excellence.

2014
Company Founded
Started as a small workshop with 3 employees, focusing on manual rolling shutters for local shops.
2016
First Industrial Project
Completed our first large-scale industrial shutter installation, marking our entry into the industrial sector.
2018
Automation Division Launched
Introduced motorized shutters with remote controls and safety features, expanding our service offerings.
2020
500th Installation
