The Real Problem

Why Most Shop Owners End Up
Paying Twice for Their Shutters

Bad shutters cost you more than money — they cost you sleep, security, and repeat repair bills. Here's what we hear from our clients every week.

🔓
Shutter Got Broken Into
"A cheap shutter saved me ₹3,000. The break-in cost me ₹80,000." Thin-gauge shutters can be lifted out of their guide channels in under 30 seconds with basic tools. Anti-lift channels and proper anchoring aren't optional — they're essential.
⚙️
Shutter Stops Working Constantly
"The motor fails every monsoon." Cheap motors, wrong spring tension, or poor installation leads to jammed shutters at the worst times — festival season, late at night, during deliveries. Downtime kills daily operations.
💸
Quoted Low, Charged High Later
"They quoted ₹15,000 then added ₹8,000 for 'extras' after installation." Beware installers who quote rock-bottom prices — the motor, controller, bottom bar, and guide channels mysteriously become chargeable line items.
